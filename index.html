This page visualizes 5,519 images from a search for "Fourth of July" on the Digital Public Library of America (DPLA), which aggregates cultural heritage metadata from thousands of institutions.

This is a WebGL visualization of how the Inception Convolutional Neural Network, trained on ImageNet 2012, perceives "relatedness," and then a mapping of that the computed relationships between images into 3-dimensional space using UMAP algorithm such that similar images appear proximate to one another.

Except for a few small modifications, this is based on the work and code published by the Yale Digital Humanities Labs in their PixPlot repository, which I learned about through an excellent video presentation from a Coalition for Networked Information (CNI) project briefing.
